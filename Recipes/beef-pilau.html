Beef Pilau

Serves 3 people

Swahili beef pilau

Growing up in Coast, Mombasa, Pilau was everything.As a small girl i used to enjoy my mother cook for us delicious pilau using unique steps and art which made me curious to learn and be able to cook on my own.Through my mother amazings skills i now became the best pilau chef in our beautiful home which made pilau to be part of my favorite food and i would confidently enjoy cooking it for my siblings

Ingredients

  • 1 piece of ginger
  • Half a garlic
  • Pilau masala
  • 2 royco cubes
  • 1 sachet of tomato paste
  • 2 medium size chopped onions
  • 2 glasses Basmat rice
  • 1 Kg beef meat
  • Clean water
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

How to make Swahili beef pilau

  1. Toast and blend the pilau masala:strong> In a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Brown the onions by frying it in oil until it caramelizes. This should take about 10 to 15 minutes. Stir in the Garlic and ginger.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste and cook till it dissolves. Then stir in the royco cubes.
  4. Add 4 glasses of clean waterand bring everything to a rolling boil
  5. Stir in the ricethen cover tightly.You can use an aluminum foil to cover it first, this will help keep the steam in. Then place the lid over it and leave to cook on a low heat for 20 minutes until the liquid has been absorbed.
  6. Serve hot!You can add Kachumbari and a piece of banana.You will definately enjoy your meal
